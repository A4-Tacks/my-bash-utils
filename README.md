replline
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Quickly and repeatedly execute certain commands, with independent history

<details>
<summary>Show Code</summary>

```
$ replline -a jq -n
REPL for jq -n
jq> .
null
jq> range(7)|.*3
0
3
6
9
12
15
18
jq> range(2;7)|.*3
6
9
12
15
18
jq> [range(2;7)|.*3]
[
  6,
  9,
  12,
  15,
  18
]
jq>
```

</details>
